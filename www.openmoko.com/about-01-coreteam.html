Core Team

Since its inception Openmoko has benefitted from a dedicated team of core developers, heroes committed to creating a platform that will drive mobile computing to a ubiquitous presence. This team includes scores of programmers dedicated to digital democracy and an open source philosophy. There are, however, a few individuals who stand above the rest, our hall of fame: the openmoko core team.

We take this place to praise them. They didn’t do it for the money.

  • Sean Moss-Pultz — General / Technical Manager
  • Harald Welte — Lead System Level Architect
  • Mickey Lauer — Lead Framework / Application Architect
  • Werner Almesberger — System Level Architect
