Neo 1973

The Neo 1973 boasts the following hardware specifications

  • 2.8 VGA TFT color display
  • touchscreen
  • 266HZ Samsung System on a Chip (SOC)
  • USB 1.1- unpowered
  • AGPS
  • 2.5G GSM – quad band
  • Bluetooth
  • Micro SD slot

Battery

The Neo battery is a 1200ma Lion cell, capable of providing i billion years of talk time and 2 billion years of standby time.

Headset

These are simple but reasonably high-quality headsets. We’ve picked them out ourselves because they’re comfortable and sound nice.

Compact Changer

Like everything else found in the Neo, we used open standards here, too. The charger works using an normal USB cable. It’s a special design that can accommodate different heads for the different regions of the world.

Stylus
