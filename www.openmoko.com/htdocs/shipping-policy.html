Shipping Policy

Domestic Shipping

Orders within the United States are shipped by UPS, which takes 3-5 business days depending on your location in the US. Your order will usually ship within 1-3 business days after you receive an emailed confirmation of your order. Openmoko USA does not ship to PO boxes. We will make all efforts to ship your order within 3-7 business days of receipt. We do not ship on Saturdays and Sundays. All orders placed on Saturday and Sunday will be processed that following Monday. During the holidays and Sundays, our order processing time may increase. Openmoko USA charges sales tax for orders shipped to California. Recipients of orders shipped to other states are responsible for complying with state and local tax laws.
