Openmoko to Present FreeRunner Embedded Mobile Platform
at Special Session During Embedded Systems Conference

 

Five FreeRunner Mobile Phones to be Given to Session Attendees

 

WHO: Openmoko Inc, http://www.openmoko.com
WHAT: The maker of the world's first totally open mobile phone, will discuss its FreeRunner mobile phone platform that provides a quick way for embedded developers to create mobile products at the Embedded Systems Conference.
WHEN: Tuesday, March 31 at 1:30pm
WHERE: ESC Theatre on the show floor

 

DETAILS: Sean Moss-Pultz, CEO of Openmoko, will talk about the FreeRunner mobile platform in an interview with Bill Gatliff, Contributing Editor for Embedded Systems Programming Magazine, and a presenter and member of the Advisory Panel for the EExploded CAD of OpenMoko Neo phonembedded Systems .

 

Following the interview, TechInsights, organizers of the Embedded Systems Conference, and Openmoko will give away an Openmoko FreeRunner to five audience members. Openmoko is also offering a show special, reducing the price of the FreeRunner from $399 to $299 for 30 days. The Openmoko FreeRunner Show Special will be available March 26 to April 26 at http://us.direct.openmoko.com/.

 

About Openmoko
Openmoko is the commercial and community driven effort with a mission to create open mobile products that empower developers and consumers to personalize the devices, much like a computer, in any way they see fit. Openmoko is dedicated to helping innovators bring freedom and flexibility to consumer electronics and vertical market devices. www.Openmoko.com.
