System Administator

Openmoko is looking to immediately hire a talented system administrator to join our team and manage our rapidly growing office and server infrastructure. You should possess high attention to detail, a strong customer service ethic, and good people skills. You must be comfortable in a self-managed environment where you seek out challenges through creatively developing both new solutions and improvements to existing systems.

In general, we look for broad creative and technical skills, rather than specific vocational experience. We concentrate on finding people with breadth, depth, communication skills and the ability to collaborate. If you have those attributes, we can teach you the specific tools.

Job location: Taipei, Taiwan

  • Required Experience:
  • BS/BA, or equivalent work experience
  • System Administrator level expertise with UNIX and/or Linux operating systems
  • Strong networking skills (mainly for debugging and troubleshooting)
  • Strong understanding of web and mail based technologies (http, https, xml, smtp, imap, pop)
  • Strong troubleshooting and systems sizing skills
  • Extremely strong team ethic and customer support focus
  • English language skills highly desired
