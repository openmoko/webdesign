Latest Career Opportunities

Openmoko has a mix of world famous Free Software developers, proven mobile hardware engineers, innovative marketers, and a DJ from Japan, all dedicated to crafting the world’s premier open mobile devices.

Creative people are the bedrock of our business. Our culture is unique: we focus on customers, quality, and freedom. We believe cutting-edge ideas trump cutting-edge technology.

We pride ourselves in our down-to-earth atmosphere cultivated in the love of our work. If you are a creative individual, passionate about shifting the mobile space, Openmoko wants you!

Contact hr@openmoko.com
The current positions:

Software Engineer

Openmoko is looking to immediately hire talented software engineers to join our team to help create and develop Open Source software for mobile devices. The position requires a deep understanding of software application engineering, an ability to collaborate in a distributed cross-functional team of world-class developers, and skill in designing and implementing robust, easy to maintain code. We aim to deliver high quality software in a fast paced, dynamic, deadline oriented environment.

In general, we look for broad creative and technical skills, rather than the ability to program in one language over another. We concentrate on finding people with breadth, depth, communication skills and the ability to collaborate. If you have those attributes, we can teach you the specific tools.

Requirements
* 5+ years programming experience (may include private projects)
* Strong hands-on knowledge of Open Source technologies
* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent
* Ability to extend existing Open Source projects
* English language skills highly desired

Electronic Engineer

Openmoko is looking for an electronic engineer to join a top notch team with responsibilities for the design and implementation of the digital and analog electronics in future open mobile devices.

An obvious passion for crafting mobile products is essential, alongside solid analog and digital electronic skills. Ideally you would have already built and shipped portable consumer electronic products.

Duties include architecture input, schematic creation / review, prototype bring-up and debug, hardware bug tracking, functional verification, signal characterization, and manufacturing support.

This position is open for all levels of skill from a junior engineer willing to learn quickly to a senior engineer.

Required Experience:
* BS/MS EE or equivalent required - you must have great EE fundamentals
* Broad experience: both digital and analog, minimum 2 years.
* Experience with prototyping and bring-up of designs
* Experience in the following areas is advantageous: system design, digital logic, memory busses, ASIC’s, processor selection, low-power design and implementation, I2C/SPI/UARTs, various communication protocols, displays, analog integration, RF integration issues, GPS.
* English language skills highly desired

Operations Head

You will report to our CEO and be part of our Senior Management Team.
We will count on you to build and deliver all of our new products.

You will be responsible for all aspects of our manufacturing including: product cost, yields, quality, reliability, on-time delivery, manufacturing cycle times, and customer service.

You should have 10 to 15 years experience in running mobile phone production lines. We want someone who has worked their way up in manufacturing, not some executive who has taken a lateral move into a top manufacturing spot.

Required Experience:
* BS required in operations management or in an engineering discipline such as electronics, mechanical, industrial or manufacturing.
* Excellent written and spoken English and Chinese
* 10 to 15 years minimum manufacturing experience
* Hands-on production line/ factory management experience
* Must have managed groups of more than 15 people
* Must have experience working with American electronics firms
* Global supply chain experience
* Purchasing and price negotiation experience
* Warehouse management experience
* Production planning experience
