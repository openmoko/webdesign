Contribute

Write Code

Please help Openmoko by contributing code. Sources are available through SVN and our Wiki. Ongoing discussions happen in the Mailing Lists.

Report Bugs

Before we can fix anything, we have to know what’s wrong. Please help by reporting bugs to the Openmoko Trac.

Share Projects

If you’re writing software with Openmoko, please share your projects on Openmoko Projects. Your inspiration and expertise is the engine that drives our open initiative.

Get Involved

The Community Portal is a place to get involved. Dig deep in our vault of resources, or provide a helping hand to newbies in the Mailing Lists.
