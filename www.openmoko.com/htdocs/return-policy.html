Return Policy

  • All returns must be authorized. To receive authorization for a return, you must contact customer service at support@openmoko.com to obtain a Return Validation number known as an RV number. If a unit arrives DOA (dead on arrival) we will replace the unit free of charge.
  • All items must be returned in a new condition. All original documents and packaging must be included. Also include the original invoice with the RV# written on it.
  • Only DOA products will be subject to return.
  • If you were to receive a defective item, you must notify us within 14 business days of when you received the product to qualify for a return or exchange. If you fail to provide us with a prompt notification, we will not validate your return or exchange. Once you receive your Return Validation number, you must than ship the item(s) back within the specified time period (14 business days) to receive an exchange or the RV# will become null and void. You will be responsible for the return shipping costs. We do not refund shipping costs on returns and or exchanges, unless there was a processing error on our behalf. We are not responsible for lost or damaged return packages. We will notify you via e-mail once your return has been processed.
