Mehanical Engineer

As a member of a cross-functional design team at Openmoko, you will help to create the next generation of the world's most open mobile devices and accessories. You will conceive, design, and bring into production products that will re-define the mobile experience. You will work closely with many different cross-functional teams including Industrial Design, Packaging, Safety, Hardware Engineering, and Marketing.

Job location: Taipei, Taiwan

  • Required Experience:
  • BSME required, MS Preferred.
  • 2-10 years experience in product design.
  • Requires demonstrated ability in plastic, sheetmetal, and cast part design.
  • 3-D CAD skills essential.
  • English language skills highly desired.
