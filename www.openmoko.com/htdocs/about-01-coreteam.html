Core Team

Since its inception Openmoko was architected by a dedicated team of core developers, heroes committed to creating a platform that will drive mobile computing to an open, ubiquitous, presence. This team includes scores of programmers dedicated to digital democracy and an open source philosophy. There are, however, a few individuals who stand above the rest, our hall of fame: the openmoko core team.

We take this place to praise them. They didn't do it for the money.

  • Sean Moss-Pultz -- Program Manager
  • Mickey Lauer -- Lead Framework / Application Architect
  • Werner Almesberger -- System Level Architect

For the core team pushing this project, an open phone is not really even a product. It's the very embodiment of their vision of technology. They absolutely, passionately, believe that something as fundamental to our lives as the mobile phone must be opened.
