DBoard

Like Make Magazine we believe “If you can’t open it, you don’t own it”. Neo’s are built for you to open. You can take the casing apart and get at the PCB. Opt for the DBoard and we’ll give you a screwdriver and a plastic guitar pick to make opening this device even easier. Go ahead, open your Neo.
